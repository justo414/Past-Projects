How you access life insurance is up to you

All Australians should be able to access life insurance. But everyone is different.

That's why you can get TAL life insurance directly from us or through a financial adviser. You can also access cover through your super fund or employer, which means for millions of Australians, TAL could well be their insurance partner.

Each option has different advantages, so you can make the choice that's best for you.
